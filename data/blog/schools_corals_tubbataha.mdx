One of the more stunning sites at Tubbataha is called Staghorn Point which is located in the South Atoll.

<Image
  src="/static/images/tubbataha_schools_corals/TubbatahaStaghorn.jpeg"
  width={800}
  height={600}
  alt="Map of Tubbataha Dive Sites"
/>

As the name indicates, the site has fields of healthy staghorn corals, soft corals, and schools of fish (jacks to be specific).
